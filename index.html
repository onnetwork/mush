About Us

Mushambahuro is a beautiful resort located in Glaudina along Bulawayo Rd is 17km from the city. Its Very Close to the very popular Pamuzinda. We are 5 minutes away from the Lion and Cheetah park ,Snake World and Harare Bird gardens.Lake Chivero is 12 minutes away. So if you are into wild life, Fishing or just relaxing Mushambahuro is the place for you!
